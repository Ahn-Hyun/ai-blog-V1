# Serena Williams Discloses Biometric Data and Strategic Health Pivot

In late 2024, **Serena Williams** publicly disclosed specific biometric data revealing the impact of her recent health regimen, marking a significant pivot from her days as a professional athlete to a focus on longevity and internal health optimization. At the age of 43, the retired tennis champion released these details alongside a high-profile commercial partnership, sparking a broader conversation regarding metabolic health, obesity management, and the intersection of celebrity influence and healthcare.

This disclosure comes at a time when Williams is expanding her business footprint through venture capital and telehealth endorsements. The release of her medical data specifically concerning the use of GLP-1 medication provides a quantitative look at her post-retirement physical state compared to her active years. As she navigates this new chapter, the overlap between her personal health choices and her family's investment interests has created a complex narrative combining medical advocacy with corporate strategy.

### TL;DR
*   **Significant Health Metrics:** Williams reported a 34-pound weight loss and a 30% reduction in total cholesterol (from 185 mg/dL to 129 mg/dL) using GLP-1 medication [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).
*   **Orthopedic Motivation:** The primary driver for the treatment was reducing load on her knee joints to mitigate pain from decades of high-impact sports, rather than aesthetics alone [People.com](https://people.com/serena-williams-i-am-at-my-healthiest-with-glp-1s-exclusive-11893854).
*   **Business Integration:** Williams is the face of the "Healthier on Ro" campaign; the partnership aligns with her husband Alexis Ohanian's role as an investor and board member of Ro [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).
*   **Venture Capital Success:** Her firm, Serena Ventures, manages a portfolio of 67 companies with eight unicorns and notable exits like Coinbase, signaling her active transition to the boardroom [Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/venture-capital/serena-ventures/__7605coX0ItAWItyUQrGM1AHxclmcznA35EExPFVkZjg).

### Biometric Data and Health Optimization

The specific metrics provided by Williams offer a direct contrast between her current health status and her baseline during her final years of competition. According to medical data shared by Williams, her total cholesterol was recorded at 185 mg/dL in 2021, a time when she was still actively competing in Grand Slam tournaments. Following her recent treatment, that figure dropped significantly to 129 mg/dL. Williams noted that some of her current health markers are superior to those recorded when she was winning major titles, challenging the assumption that peak athletic performance equates to peak metabolic health [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).

Williams emphasized that the primary goal of her transformation was internal health optimization rather than external appearance. She highlighted the 30% reduction in her overall cholesterol levels as a key outcome of the treatment. This reduction correlates with her claim that the regimen has lowered her lifetime risk of heart disease by a relative 70%. Describing the physical change, the retired champion reported a total weight loss of 34 lbs, stating that she feels "lighter" and "healthier" than she felt during parts of her professional career [People.com](https://people.com/serena-williams-i-am-at-my-healthiest-with-glp-1s-exclusive-11893854).

### Orthopedic Drivers for Medication

Beyond cardiovascular metrics, Williams cited orthopedic preservation as a specific motivation for utilizing pharmaceutical intervention. She stated that reducing the physical load on her knee joints was a critical factor in her decision to start the medication, aiming to mitigate pain accumulated over decades of high-impact sports. This shift marks a distinct move from performance-based training to longevity-focused medical intervention [People.com](https://people.com/serena-williams-i-am-at-my-healthiest-with-glp-1s-exclusive-11893854).

During her career, joint management was largely handled through physical therapy and conditioning; however, in retirement, she has utilized medication to achieve similar relief through weight reduction. This medical intervention aims to preserve mobility and comfort without the high-impact demands of professional athletic training. Williams expressed that the transformation has allowed her to feel her "healthiest," prioritizing longevity and mobility in her post-retirement life [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).

### Strategic Partnerships and Family Ties

The release of this private health data coincided with a commercial partnership between Williams and the telehealth company Ro. Williams is the face of the "Healthier on Ro" campaign, which includes a Super Bowl commercial appearance intended to destigmatize obesity treatments. This collaboration effectively monetizes her patient experience, transforming a private medical decision into a public brand endorsement [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).

The collaboration has a familial business link, as Williams' husband, Alexis Ohanian, is both an investor in and a board member of Ro. This context clarifies the commercial infrastructure supporting the public dissemination of Williams' specific cholesterol and weight loss statistics. While Williams advocates for the clinical benefits she experienced, the overlap between her endorsement and her husband's fiduciary role adds a layer of complex business strategy to the campaign, suggesting that her advocacy for the medication is intertwined with her family's investment interests in the digital health sector [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/serena-williams-weight-loss-drug-rcna256202).

### Serena Ventures and Capital Allocation

Williams' focus on health optimization occurs alongside her expanding role in venture capital, requiring sustained energy outside the structure of athletic training. While she is best known for her athletic dominance, her investment firm, Serena Ventures, has carved out a distinct and measurable position in the financial sector. Based in San Francisco, the firm operates with a lean team of seven, including three partners, yet manages a substantial portfolio of assets. Recent data indicates Serena Ventures oversees investments in 67 companies, signaling a shift from celebrity endorsements to active capital allocation [Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/venture-capital/serena-ventures/__7605coX0ItAWItyUQrGM1AHxclmcznA35EExPFVkZjg).

The firm's track record includes specific liquidity events and valuation milestones that validate its investment thesis. Portfolio data indicates holdings in eight "unicorns"startups valued at over $1 billionhighlighting an ability to identify high-growth potential in early stages. Furthermore, the firm has realized returns through nine acquisitions and one initial public offering (IPO), specifically the cryptocurrency exchange platform Coinbase. Recent investment activity suggests a continued interest in sports and lifestyle verticals, evidenced by the firm's participation in the Series B funding round for Unrivaled Basketball [Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/venture-capital/serena-ventures/__7605coX0ItAWItyUQrGM1AHxclmcznA35EExPFVkZjg).

### Speculation Regarding a Return to Tennis

The physical improvements resulting from GLP-1 usage have fueled speculation regarding a potential return to professional tennis. While *People* quotes Williams stating she has "no intention of coming out of retirement," she acknowledged that the thought persists despite pressure from her sister, Venus. Conversely, *NBC News* characterized her stance as more ambiguous, describing her response to the question as "not a yes or a no." Regardless of future athletic endeavors, Williams maintains that her current regimen has placed her in a superior state of health compared to her competitive years [People.com](https://people.com/serena-williams-i-am-at-my-healthiest-with-glp-1s-exclusive-11893854).
