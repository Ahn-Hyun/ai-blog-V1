### Performance Against Houston
In a 111-99 victory over the Houston Rockets, Harper delivered a highly efficient performance from the second unit. The rookie recorded 16 points, four rebounds, three assists, and one block while playing 22 minutes. This well-rounded stat line marked a noteworthy contribution for the first-round pick, who continues to carve out a role in the Spurs' rotation [1](https://www.rotowire.com/basketball/headlines/dylan-harper-news-heats-up-with-16-points-off-bench-516328), [2](https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/basketball/news/spurs-dylan-harper-heats-up-with-16-points-off-bench/).

Harper's offensive impact was defined by his accuracy inside the arc, finishing 8-for-11 from the field. Although he missed both attempts from three-point range, his ability to convert high-percentage two-point opportunities provided a necessary spark. This efficiency reinforced his utility as a scoring option who does not require perimeter volume to be effective [1](https://www.rotowire.com/basketball/headlines/dylan-harper-news-heats-up-with-16-points-off-bench-516328).

### Establishing a Statistical Baseline
The 16-point effort against the Rockets stands as Harper's most prolific scoring display since a win in Indiana on January 2. While he has remained active in the rotation, surpassing the 15-point threshold highlights a peak in his recent offensive trajectory. This resurgence demonstrates that the rookie is capable of capitalizing on his minutes to significantly impact the game [1](https://www.rotowire.com/basketball/headlines/dylan-harper-news-heats-up-with-16-points-off-bench-516328).

Leading up to the Rockets matchup, Harper had already established a baseline of consistent production. Over his previous eight appearances, he averaged 10.3 points, 3.6 rebounds, and 3.6 assists in approximately 21.5 minutes per game. This steady profile indicates that the efficient outing against Houston was an elevation of his standard play rather than an anomaly [1](https://www.rotowire.com/basketball/headlines/dylan-harper-news-heats-up-with-16-points-off-bench-516328).
