# Intel Announces Corporate Match for "Trump Accounts" Amid Federal Push

On January 27, 2026, Intel publicly committed to a new employee benefit involving "Trump Accounts," marking one of the first major corporate endorsements of the initiative. The company stated it will **match** the governments contribution to these accounts, establishing a corporate incentive alongside the federal program [[1]](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/27/intel-says-it-will-match-governments-trump-accounts-contribution.html). This announcement arrives as the administration launches a concerted effort to integrate these accounts into standard household financial planning.

### Federal Push During Tax Season
The federal initiative has been positioned as a priority for households during the current financial cycle. President Trump is actively urging families to establish "Trump Accounts" to coincide with the start of tax season, suggesting a structural link between annual tax filings and the activation of the accounts [[2]](https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/trump-urge-families-open-trump-accounts-tax-season/story?id=129611160).
