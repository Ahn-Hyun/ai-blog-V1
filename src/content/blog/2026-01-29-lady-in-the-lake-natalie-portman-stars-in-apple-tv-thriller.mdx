# Lady in the Lake: Natalie Portman Stars in Apple TV+ Limited Series

Natalie Portman stars in *Lady in the Lake*, a seven-episode limited series released on Apple TV+ in July 2024. Adapted from Laura Lippman's best-selling novel, the noir thriller is set in 1966 Baltimore. In addition to playing the lead role, Portman serves as an executive producer on the project, which was created and directed by Alma Harel.

### TL;DR
*   **Premiere Date:** The series debuted globally on July 19, 2024, with a two-episode launch [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].
*   **Format:** It is a seven-episode limited series [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].
*   **Creative Team:** Alma Harel served as creator, writer, and director, while Natalie Portman starred and executive produced [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].
*   **Setting:** The drama takes place in 1966 Baltimore [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].

### Premiere and Release Schedule
Apple TV+ launched the series globally on Friday, July 19, 2024, with the first two episodes. This debut followed a red carpet premiere hosted by the streaming platform. The remaining chapters followed a weekly release schedule every Friday, concluding the run on August 23, 2024 [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].

### Narrative and Genre
The series serves as a television adaptation of the novel by Laura Lippman. By translating the book into a serialized format, the production constructs a period-specific mystery within the noir thriller genre [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].

### Production and Creative Leadership
Filmmaker Alma Harel led the project as creator, writer, director, and executive producer. Natalie Portman also executive produced the series alongside her producing partner, Sophie Mas [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].

### Conclusion
*Lady in the Lake* marks a significant collaboration between Alma Harel and Natalie Portman, who balanced acting duties with executive production. The limited series leveraged the global reach of Apple TV+ to deliver its contained thriller narrative, finishing its broadcast run in August 2024 [[1](https://macdailynews.com/2024/07/12/apple-tv-hosts-red-carpet-premiere-for-new-series-lady-in-the-lake-starring-natalie-portman/)].
